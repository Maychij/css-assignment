Capacity Bay, Inc., is a practical and hands-on eLearning platform and organization established to revolutionize learning and workforce development training for U.S. veterans, transitioning service members, new Americans, immigrants, refugees, and others in areas of substantial unemployment and technology-deprived communities such as sub-Saharan Africa and other parts of the world. We also provide integrated IT consulting services for individuals, businesses, and government agencies Capacitybay currently runs a software programming language class that will last for nine months. For each programming language, there are specified tutors, otherwise known as faculties. The languages taught are as follows: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery, etc.
